It takes great wisdom to know when you are supposed to be living life differently.

Honoring, reconnecting with, and honing your immense inner wisdom is the crux of my coaching approach. With a sense of growing peace, you will embark on your journey. I'll 'meet you where you are', listen deeply, use (and teach) many guided inquiry and mind-body techniques to support you in your metamorphosis.

I am a Masters prepared holistic health practitioner. I have learned (and am still learning) from the very best: I am completing my Life Coaching certification with Martha Beck, Inc, none other than Oprah's own personal life coach. I have worked in the field of health and wellness for over 30 years, leaving a whirlwind corporate life behind. I now live on the CA coast, coach, lead local support groups, volunteer, love to open water kayak and am living a life I love. I would be honored to guide you along your journey towards a life you'll love, too.
